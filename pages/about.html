about

We serve a wide variety of condiments and spices to enhance any meal!

Combining a wholesome family recipe with the familiar spicy sensation, K'Sause offers a wide range of condiments & flavor enhancers to add a unique and satisfying flavour to complement any meal.

K'Sauce produces flavors which varies from a tickle on the tongue to a full on explosion of hot flavor in your mouth.

K'Suace owes its unique flavor and guarantee of satisfaction on a recipe passed down a generation and perfected over 15 years by founder Kutlwano Matseke

Founder: Kutlwano Matseke

I have been cooking since I was 8 years and have always had a special relationship with flavor allowing me to perfect various enhancers that maximize the familiar spicy sensation & satisfaction it brings the taste buds.
